About FindBombs

Welcome to FindBombs, your gateway to discovering the vibrant world of urban art in Greece!

This site showcases graffiti and provides access to photo albums of various walls. Explore the beauty of urban art in Athens, Iraklion, and Thessaloniki!

Our Mission

We aim to document, preserve, and celebrate the incredible street art that adorns the walls of Greek cities. From wildstyle masterpieces to thought-provoking murals, discover the stories behind each piece.
